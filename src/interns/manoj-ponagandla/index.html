Most of the technology that we are developing today is focused on making lives easier and comfortable. While this is true in many cases, there are a few where technology is complicating things for many people. Direct mail is one such area, and you, the Lob team, are focusing on solving that problem. Lob's motive to bridge the gap between traditional mail and modern businesses is fascinating. I would love to contribute to this mission in a way I could. By that I mean, working as a Software Developer at Lob.

I always carried a purpose-driven mentality and get highly inspired by such missions. That is one of the reasons why I want to work with you. Being a software engineer in this software era, where a piece of code can connect multiple pieces of a puzzle to solve a problem, My motivation is to look for those problems and contribute to the solutions and make the world a better place.

While I addressed these professional reasons, Lob's ethical concern towards the environment is a significant factor that influenced me. I am highly particular when it comes to the planet's eco-system. I changed my lifestyle in a great way to reduce my carbon footprint as much as possible. By working with the Lob team, I want to use my knowledge in various skills to help improve the product, and also gain more expertise in the areas I already excel in, all while being equally responsible to the life on the planet.

At last, I am personally connected to the blue color accent across the company's visual assets. Yes, I love the color Blue, and I only realized that in a funny way. That's a story I'm hoping to get to share with you soon.
