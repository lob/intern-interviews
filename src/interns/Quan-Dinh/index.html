About Me

My name is Quan Dinh, a CS Junior at the University of Texas at Dallas. I interned last summer at RealPage, Inc., which specialized in making data analytics software for real estate companies. I attended several hackathons before COVID-19 and won 1st place at HackUTD 2019 for making Alexa skills.

For an internship, I would love to learn about the company's technology stack. I also love to learn from mentors, professionals with years of valuable experience who can teach me much more than what school has to offer.

I want to become a software engineer because I have been fascinated by technology for a long time. I dream of making software that can impact a large group of people. Becoming a software engineer intern at a great company is a vital step toward that dream.
